Dear Dr. Jürgen Meister,

I am writing in response to your advertisement for the vacancy at OFFIS as Software and System Architect. I believe my experience, skills and qualification are not only an asset, but an ideal match to the requirements of your operation.

I currently live in Göttingen, where I very recently finished my M.Sc. studies in Applied Computer Science at the Georg-August University. I have specialized in Scientific Computing. During my studies I took three consecutive courses on Mathematical Signal and Image Processing at the Institute of Numerical and Applied Mathematics, followed by a masters thesis in the same field. I am therefore well-acquainted with applying mathematical ideas and relationships in a software engineering context.

Presently, I work at the Institute of Propulsion Technology at the German Aerospace Center, where I wrote my master's thesis in cooperation with my university. In the course of this thesis project I developed a turbine blade cooling geometry generator called CoolingGen. The German Aerospace Center have their own CAD kernel. The small set of input parameters used to design blades yields a semantic interface between geometry creation and physical function. I was able to prove my deep mathematical understanding of linear algebra and computer-aided design and my implementation talent in C/C++ and Python. To assess the quality of certain features of the software, the project CoolingGen also required me to communicate features and requirements with an interdisciplinary team, which was not only pleasant, but always yielded much productive value. 

CoolingGen does not only enable its' user to build digital twins of jet engines, but also modify existing turbine blade cooling. Therefore, my previous experience at the interface between experiment and simulation makes me the perfect candidate. I am looking forward to working within a team of experts dedicated to the given objectives.

Even though I whole-heartedly enjoy my current work, I feel that I have to move on. While I am certain that I have what it takes, I acknowledge and appreciate the challenges ahead. I am looking forward to hearing from you.
